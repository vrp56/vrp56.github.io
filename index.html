About Me

My name is Viraj Patel. I am a Master's student in Comuter Science at Mississippi State University. My areas of study are Computer Graphics, specifically with Augmented and Virtual Reality, and Artificial Intelligence. I am working on my thesis where I investigate driver performance, situation awareness, and cognitive load at differnt levels of partial autonomy with dynamic task allocation. I am comparing data I find in my research to data collected from a similar study done in a web-based desktop driving simulator. More details of this can be found under the Portfolio tab below.
